About Us

In today's fast-paced remote work environment, maintaining productivity can be a challenge. The Remote Work Productivity Tracker is designed to empower remote workers by providing the tools they need to track and enhance their productivity effectively.

Our web app monitors work patterns, offering insights into when and how users work best. By analyzing focus time, task completion, and productivity trends, we help users identify their peak performance hours and optimize their work schedules accordingly.
